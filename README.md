Step 1: Interact with the Source
In OBS, look at your Sources list and find your shootgallery source.

Right-click on shootgallery.

Click on Interact from the menu. This will open up the live popup window where you can click and type.

Step 2: Connect Your Twitch Chat
In the Settings panel on the left side of the window, look under 1. Twitch Connection.

Click the empty white box and type in your Twitch channel name.

Click the Connect button right below it.

Step 3: Set Your Game Rules
Adjust your settings in the 2. Game Rules section:

Hit Goal: Type in the number of hits the chat needs to win (Default is 50).

Time (sec): Set how many seconds the challenge lasts (Default is 60).

Step 4: Start the Game!
Click the large green START CHALLENGE button to begin the countdown!

Step 5: (Optional) Custom Visuals & Speeds
If you want to customize the look, use the 3. Visuals & Speed section:

Use the Choose file buttons to upload your own images for the Target, Hero, and Ammo.

Change the number boxes (like Ammo Speed, Target Speed, or asset sizes) to adjust the game difficulty.
